Frequently Asked Questions

Is FusePDF really free?

Yes! FusePDF is completely free to use with no hidden costs, registration, or limitations on file processing.

Are my files uploaded to your servers?

No. All PDF processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

What file size limits exist?

Processing is limited by your device's memory. Most devices can handle PDFs up to 100MB without issues.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! Once the page loads, you can use FusePDF without an internet connection.

What browsers are supported?

FusePDF works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
