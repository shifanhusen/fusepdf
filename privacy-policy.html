Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At FusePDF, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our PDF processing tools. The most important thing to understand is that all PDF processing happens entirely within your browser, meaning your documents never leave your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your PDF Files: All PDF processing occurs client-side in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Document Content: We cannot see, access, or store the content of your PDF files or images.
  • Personal Documents: Since processing is local, we have no access to any sensitive information in your files.
  • Account Information: We don't require registration, so we don't collect names, emails, or passwords.
  • File Metadata: We don't collect information about your file names, sizes, or creation dates.

Information We May Collect

  • Usage Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website (page views, time spent, general location).
  • Technical Information: Basic browser information (browser type, operating system) for compatibility purposes.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies for theme preferences and website functionality.

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website's functionality and user experience
  • Understand which features are most useful to our users
  • Ensure compatibility across different browsers and devices
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies to show you personalized ads. You can control ad personalization in your Google Ad Settings.

Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to analyze website usage. This service uses cookies to track visitor behavior anonymously.

CDN Services

We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load JavaScript libraries (PDF-lib, PDF.js) for faster performance.

Data Security

Since your documents never leave your device, they are as secure as your own computer. We implement standard web security practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all website communications
  • No server-side file storage or processing
  • Regular security updates to our web platform
  • Minimal data collection practices

Your Rights and Choices

  • Cookie Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Ad Preferences: You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ad Settings
  • Analytics Opt-out: You can use browser extensions to block Google Analytics
  • Complete Privacy: Since processing is local, your documents remain completely private

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

FusePDF is designed to work globally. Since all processing happens in your browser, your documents never cross international borders through our service. Any analytics data collected may be processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we collect minimal information, most updates will be to clarify our practices or comply with legal requirements.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@fusepdf.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Page

Key Takeaway: FusePDF processes your documents entirely within your browser. We never see, store, or have access to your PDF files or their content. Your privacy is protected by design.
