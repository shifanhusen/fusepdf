All Innovitech tools are designed with privacy as a core principle. Most processing happens locally in your browser, and we collect minimal data only when necessary.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Forms: When you contact us through any Innovitech project, we collect your name, email address, subject, message, and the project you're contacting about.
  • Multiplayer Gaming: In TurboType Rally, we temporarily store your chosen display name and game session data.

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand how our tools are used (pages visited, features used, general location).
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, and IP address for security and optimization purposes.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and may use advertising cookies if you consent.

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • File Contents: PDF processing in FusePDF happens entirely in your browser. We never see or store your files.
  • Personal Documents: No uploaded files are transmitted to our servers.
  • Keystroke Logging: In TurboType Rally, only progress percentages are shared, not actual typed content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Contact Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support across all projects.
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve our tools and develop new features.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and protect against fraud or abuse.
  • Communications: Send important updates about services (with opt-out options).

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Firebase Infrastructure: Contact form data is stored securely using Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption.
  • Temporary Data: Gaming session data is automatically deleted when sessions end.
  • Access Control: Only authorized administrators can access contact form submissions.
  • Data Retention: Contact submissions are retained for customer service purposes and may be deleted upon request.

4. Third-Party Services

Innovitech uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Database and authentication services (subject to Google's privacy policy).
  • Google AdSense: Advertising services with privacy-focused ad personalization options.
  • Vercel: Hosting platform for reliable and fast service delivery.
  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics to improve our services (can be opted out).

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what personal data we have about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements).
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential cookies and marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us through our contact form or email.

6. Project-Specific Privacy Notes

FusePDF Tools

  • All PDF processing occurs locally in your browser
  • No files are uploaded to our servers
  • Processed files remain on your device only

TurboType Rally

  • Multiplayer sessions use temporary room codes
  • Only progress percentages and display names are shared
  • No chat logs or typed content is stored
  • Session data is automatically deleted when games end

Future Tools

  • New tools will follow the same privacy-first principles
  • Any changes to data collection will be clearly disclosed
  • Local processing will be prioritized when possible

7. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (theme preferences, session management).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our tools (Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense for relevant ads (can be disabled).

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Using services with appropriate data protection frameworks (Google Firebase, Vercel)
  • Implementing strong security measures
  • Complying with applicable international data protection laws

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice through our services or via email for significant changes

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Contact Form: innovitecho.com/contact
  • Email: innovitech0@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
